Biography

I am a third year PhD student at Laboratoire SAMM (Statistiques, Analyse, and Modélisation Multidisciplinaire) under the supervision of Pr. Pierre Latouche, Pr. Charles Bouveyron and Dr. Alain Livartowski. My main area of research is the modelisation of heterogeneous data, specifically images with text associated to them. I’m particularly interested in medical application and my PhD thesis is done in collaboration with Institut Curie where I work on the modelisation of anathomo-pathological images along with their medical reports.
