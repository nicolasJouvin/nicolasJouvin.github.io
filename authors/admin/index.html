Nicolas Jouvin

PhD Student in Applied Mathematics

SAMM

I am a third year PhD student at Laboratoire SAMM (Statistiques, Analyse, and Modélisation Multidisciplinaire) under the supervision of Pr. Pierre Latouche, Pr. Charles Bouveyron and Dr. Alain Livartowski. My main area of research is model-based clustering, probabilistic dimension reduction and variational methods. I’m particularly interested in medical application and my PhD thesis is done in collaboration with Institut Curie where I work on the modelisation of clinical data from anathomo-pathological images to textual medical reports.
